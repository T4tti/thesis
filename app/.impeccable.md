## Design Context

### Users
Corporate credit analysts, financial researchers, and investors focused on the Vietnamese market. They need a tool that feels authoritative, mathematically sound, and efficient for evaluating credit risk.

### Brand Personality
**Voice:** Authoritative, clinical, and precise.
**Tone:** Editorial and professional. It should feel like a premium financial publication (e.g., Bloomberg, Financial Times) mixed with a high-tech analytical tool.
**3-word personality:** Authoritative, Analytical, Premium.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Visual Tone:** Refined functionalism. Clean layouts with strong visual hierarchy. Use of semantic colors (IG/HY/Distressed) to guide analysis.
**Theme:** Both Light and Dark modes supported, with a focus on high contrast and legibility.
**References:** Modern financial terminals, editorial data visualizations.

### Design Principles
1. **Precision over Decoration:** Every visual element must serve a functional purpose in data interpretation.
2. **Analytical Flow:** Layouts should follow the logic of a financial audit or credit report.
3. **Contrast as Guide:** Use color and weight strategically to highlight risk and performance metrics.
4. **Editorial Integrity:** Typography and spacing should feel like a well-designed report, not just a generic dashboard.
