## Design Context

### Users
VN-Rating serves a mixed audience:
- Investment and credit analysts evaluating corporate credit risk
- Internal research users validating model behavior and data quality
- Corporate finance users assessing issuer health signals

Usage context spans both desktop and mobile, with support for quick checks and deeper analysis workflows.

### Brand Personality
Core brand voice:
- Authoritative
- Rigorous
- Impartial

Emotional goals for the interface:
- Confidence
- Trust
- Calm
- Clarity

### Aesthetic Direction
Visual direction: Editorial + analytical.

Theme strategy:
- Light-first experience with dark support

Color constraints:
- Use colors aligned with the existing VN-Rating logo and identity (brand blue-led palette).

Product/UI context inferred from codebase:
- Bilingual interface (Vietnamese and English)
- Finance-first, data-dense workflows (methodology, reports, rating tool)
- Professional and decision-support oriented presentation

### Design Principles
1. Data-first clarity: prioritize scanability, hierarchy, and interpretable outputs over decorative UI.
2. Evidence-backed confidence: present ratings, probabilities, and explanations in a transparent, auditable way.
3. Professional calm: avoid visual noise; maintain a composed, high-credibility tone suitable for risk decisions.
4. Editorial structure for complex content: use strong typographic hierarchy and section rhythm for long-form methodology and reports.
5. Bilingual consistency: preserve equivalent clarity, tone, and information density across EN/VI experiences.
